From Data to Value: The Detailing Conversation

A field force that can recite the data but not translate it loses the time-poor doctor. Detailing is won on value, not recitation. This guide moves reps from presenting to translating.

Lead with their world, not your product

  • Open with the prescriber's problem, not the product's features.
  • Translate data into what it means for their patients.
  • Ask questions; a call is a conversation, not a broadcast.

Read the call

  1. Adapt the message to the specialty and the setting.
  2. Stop when the doctor has heard enough; do not finish the brochure.
  3. Earn a next step, not just a sample dropped.

Coach the conversation, not the count

Reps drift back to reciting data under target pressure. The fix is managers coaching real calls on translation and questioning, not just checking the call count. Value is a communication skill, and it is the highest-leverage thing to build in the field.
