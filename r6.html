The Leading-Indicator Guide: Instrumenting Conversation Effort

CSAT tells you how the last conversation went. Effort tells you how the next one will go, while you can still act. Here is what to measure and where to start.

Lagging vs leading

Lagging (score-keeping)Leading (act on it)
CSAT, NPS, churnCustomer Effort Score, First Contact Resolution, repeat-contact rate, transfer rate

The one to add first

Customer Effort Score (CES). A single question after an interaction: “how easy was it to get your issue handled?” It predicts loyalty earlier and more reliably than satisfaction, and it points straight at the conversation.

How to read effort inside the conversation

  • How many times did the customer repeat themselves?
  • How many transfers or hand-offs?
  • Did the first person resolve it, or pass it on?

Each of these is created or removed by how the frontline person listens, clarifies, and resolves. Instrument the effort, build the skill that lowers it, and CSAT follows.
