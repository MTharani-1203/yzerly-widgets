The 1-Page Capability Scorecard

Completion proves attendance. These four numbers prove capability. Use this as the scorecard you take into any budget conversation.

The four numbers

MetricWhat it isHow to instrument it
Behaviour change (L3)Does the trained behaviour show up in real work?Sample real calls / emails / meetings 2-4 weeks after, score for the behaviour.
Business KPI (L4)Did the metric the training targeted move?Name the KPI before the programme; baseline it; re-measure at 60-90 days.
Manager-verifiedIndependent evidence, not self-report.Ask the manager: “show me where you saw this in their work.”
Retention past decayIs the behaviour still there at 60-90 days?Re-sample at day 60 or 90; compare to the day-14 read.

How to use it

  • Before you buy training, ask the vendor which of these four they will baseline and re-measure. If the answer is none, you are buying completion.
  • Score your current programmes 0-4. Anything under 2 is a vanity scorecard.
  • The single highest-value fix is naming the business KPI before the training, not after.
